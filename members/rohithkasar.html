Projects

GreekConnect (iOS)

If you're interested in this project, you should check out the source code.



This project is a social media app for Greek Life on UCSD campus. Users can login with their organization and keep track of private updates as well as publicly post to a feed about upcoming fundraisers and public events. Additionally, users can invite other organizations to parties and exchanges, and each user gets a push notification when this occurs. Built in Swift and backed by Google Firebase.

WhatsCookin (iOS)

We aim to push this app to the app store by the start of Fall Quarter.




This app is being developed for a startup in the Basement, an incubator on UCSD campus. The app provides a service similar to Uber Eats and Postmates, except it is Peer-to-Peer. Students can make their own food and post it on our app, and other students can browse the app and purchase homemade food from students right here on campus. We are targeting a release date of Fall Quarter.

TSE (React Native)

If you're interested in this project, you should check out the source code.


As part of the project team, TSE is developing a mobile app for the International Health Collective. It is focused on mental health and wellness and comprises of a daily check-in for data points such as mood, hours of sleep, and more. Once the user checks in, they receive in-app currency they can use to grow and take care of a plant object. The idea is that as the user takes care of the fictional plant, they simultaneously take care of their mental health.
